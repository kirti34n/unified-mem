# Research foundations

The design rules are distilled from published work. **ACE**: evolving playbooks, context-collapse and brevity-bias failure modes, hence incremental note operations and never wholesale rewrites. **MemRL / TAME**: similarity-times-utility retrieval and contribution-weighted Q updates. **CODESKILL**: verifiable rewards beat judge-only scoring. **SCM / Letta**: sleep-time consolidation (about 30% redundancy by session ten without it). **SleepGate**: stale-retrieval rate as a first-class metric. **A-MEM**: atomic Zettelkasten-style linked notes beat raw chunks. **MemFail**: memory systems fail at the write step through over-compression and bad merges, hence the verbatim rule and the arbiter. **"Context Length Alone Hurts" (ACL 2025)**: even irrelevant filler degrades performance, hence the aggressive relevance floors and the inject-nothing default. **LongMemEval**: abstention ability as a first-class measure, hence negative probes.
